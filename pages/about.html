About Data Journalism


Data journalism is a type of journalism where data is used predominantly to create stories. This reflects the increased role of data in the production and the distribution of information. The use of data journalism helps tell a complex story through the use of infographics and data visualizations which the audience will find compelling.

Our Aim


We aim to shed a light on various sectors of India like health, education, industry, commerce, infrastructure, science and technology etc. through data driven articles. Only government data and data from reliable sources will be used for this purpose. We will strive to showcase facts to our audience without any bias.
